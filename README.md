# life-in-uk-test
Some study digests and Practice Exams that helped me pass the Life in UK Test (2026)

Click on the html here in github, then click on download (right side menu). Open with Chrome/Edge/Firefox. Safari may not let you open theme, but you can try.

In the study.hmlt, the 3 dots indicate the occurence of this section/topic/fact in the actual exam. 

**Study Dots Legend:**
- 3 full dots = high occurence
- 2 full dots = medium occurence
- 1 full dot = low occurence
